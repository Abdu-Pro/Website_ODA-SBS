ABOUT US

OROMIA DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION

Welcome to our website. Oromia development association (ODA) was established in march 1993 by some concerned people of Oromia with a view to mitigating the development problems of the region by mobilizing local and external recources
It is an indigenous,non-profit making non-governmental, neutral of any political,religious and racial attitudes. Any person, legal or physical, who is in sympathy with the overall aim and objectivws of ODA can be the member of the Association. The Organization welcomes any kind of support. We accept financial and other types of materials support.

General Director of ODA Mr.Dajene Iticha


Today, more than 3 million people are benefiting from these projects. And ODA has invested more than 115 million birr for the implementation of these projects. In addition to this the association has been actively involving in humanitarian activities such as relief operation and resettlement programs. Last but not least, ODA invites all interested groups that are involving in the poverty reduction and development challengs to support association both financially and morally.
